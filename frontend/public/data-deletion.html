User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: 2026-04-23

Unravler lets you delete your account and associated data. The fastest way is from inside the app. If you cannot access your account, email us and we will help.

1. Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Log in to Unravler at https://app.unravler.com.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Go to Privacy & Data.
  4. Select Delete Account and confirm.

2. Disconnect Social Accounts

You can disconnect connected social accounts (such as Instagram) inside the app from Connected Accounts. Disconnecting stops future actions from Unravler. You may also revoke access from the social platform’s settings (for example, Meta/Facebook settings for connected apps).
