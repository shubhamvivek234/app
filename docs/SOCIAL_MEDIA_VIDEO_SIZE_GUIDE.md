## General Tips for Video

1. **Always use MP4 with H.264 codec** — universally supported across all platforms
2. **1080 x 1920 for vertical video** — the standard for Stories, Reels, TikTok, and Shorts
3. **Keep videos under 25 Mbps bitrate** — some platforms will compress aggressively
4. **Audio bitrate 128 kbps or lower** — Instagram and Threads enforce this limit
5. **Frame rate 24–30 FPS is sufficient** — higher FPS increases file size without noticeable quality improvement
6. **Custom thumbnails must be selected from video frames** on most platforms via Buffer
7. **YouTube Shorts: max 3 minutes** — this is the newest Shorts limit
8. **Bluesky has daily limits** — 25 videos per day or 10 GB total, whichever comes first
