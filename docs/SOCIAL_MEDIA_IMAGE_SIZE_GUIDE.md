## General Tips

1. **Always upload at 1080px width** — this is the universal standard across most platforms
2. **Vertical images perform better** — 4:5 (Instagram/Facebook) and 9:16 (Stories/Reels) get more engagement
3. **Use JPG or PNG** — these are universally supported across all platforms
4. **Keep important elements centered** — profile photos and banners crop differently on mobile vs desktop
5. **Respect safe zones** — leave room for UI overlays on Stories and Reels thumbnails
